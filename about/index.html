About Ann

Freezing Memories, Framing Life: A Personal Odyssey of Captured Moments

01.

Hi, I’m Ann, and photography is my passion project, a creative space where I explore the world through light, perspective, and genuine human connection. I’m drawn to authentic moments, subtle details, and the quiet beauty that often goes unnoticed. Through my phone’s lens, I seek to tell stories, evoke emotion, and create images that feel real, timeless, and meaningful.

My journey has also been shaped by my travels across Africa, including experiences in South Africa and Cape Verde, where diverse cultures, landscapes, and everyday life continue to inspire my work. These experiences influence how I see the world; rich in color, depth, and human connection, and shape the stories I choose to tell.

For me, photography is not just about taking pictures; it’s about seeing differently, appreciating the present, and preserving memories in their purest form. Each frame is a reflection of how I experience the world; honest, intentional, and deeply personal.
